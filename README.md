🚁 Water Surface Clean-Up Drone
Project Overview

The Water Surface Clean-Up Drone is an innovative unmanned aerial system designed to collect floating waste from water bodies such as rivers, lakes, ponds, reservoirs, and coastal regions. The drone utilizes a lightweight collection mechanism to capture floating plastic waste and transport it to designated disposal areas, helping reduce water pollution and improve environmental sustainability.

#Problem Statement

Plastic waste and floating debris in water bodies pose serious environmental threats to aquatic ecosystems. Traditional cleanup methods are often labor-intensive, costly, and difficult to deploy in inaccessible areas.

This project aims to develop a low-cost drone-based solution capable of collecting floating waste from water surfaces efficiently and safely.

Working Principle
The drone flies over the targeted water body.
Floating waste is captured using a lightweight collection net.
The FPV camera provides real-time monitoring to the operator.
The collected waste is transported to a designated collection point.
A servo mechanism releases the waste for disposal.
The drone returns for the next cleanup cycle.
